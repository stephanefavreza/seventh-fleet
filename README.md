### 🛡️ What is seventh-fleet?

A single flaw in a battle plan can collapse an entire star fleet. Your cloud infrastructure is no different. The rebels rely on unpredictability; we rely on absolute, flawless automation.

**seventh-fleet** is a collection of calculated, precision-guided scripts built to monitor, secure, and dominate your tenant galaxy. We do not react to anomalies; we map them, study their art, and eliminate their inefficiency.
